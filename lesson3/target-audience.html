Target Audience

The target audience for this site is people who are interested in getting invovled with the chamber of commerce, particularly those who own or operate small-medium businesses.

Personas

  1. Billy Bob: Has owned the mom and pop diner on the corner of main street for the last 10 years. After years of not wanting to participate with the Chamber of Commerce, he's changed his mind and wants to know what it's all about, and how it can help him and his business.
  2. Susan Jones: Just started a gardening store in the new part of town. After struggling for a year or so she has come to the website to see if she can network with some of the other businesses in town, and maybe get some advertising.

Scenarios

  1. My fellow business owner across the street told me about the Chamber of Commerce, but I've never heard of one. What is it, and why should I be a part of it?
  2. I forgot what day of the month our meetings are on, I'd like to look it up to make sure.
  3. Hello, I'm Susan Jones, I don't know anyone in the Chamber of Commerce, I want to get some contact information to learn more.
  4. My name is Billy Bob, I heard that there are different levels of participation with the Chamber, what are they?
  5. Who else is in the Chamber of Commerce?
